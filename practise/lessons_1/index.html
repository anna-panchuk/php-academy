MacKeeper
Is a bundled utility that includes 17 maintenance tools and services for the Mac OS X. There are several groups of tools and services, such as human assistance, security, data control, cleaning, and optimization.
The Human Assistance group contains the following services:
  • Find & Fix, where a real-life Apple Certified Expert analyzes the results of system scan and show the user what should be improved or fixed.
  • Geek on Demand, where a technical expert can answer any Mac-related question within 48 hours.
MacKeeper Features
Find & Fix
Is a full system scan with human touch. It checks your Mac for risky items and then sends a report to your Apple Certified Support Professional, who then offers remote fixes for any found items.
Geek on Demand
Is a service that lets you ask any question about your Mac and get guaranteed technical assistance from Apple Certified Support Professionals within 48 hours.
